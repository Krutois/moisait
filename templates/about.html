About VoiceFlow

VoiceFlow is an innovative solution for people with hearing impairments, converting spoken words into text in real time. We strive to make communication accessible to everyone.

Powered by the Web Speech API, our service offers support for three languages (Russian, Kazakh, English), saves your transcription history, and provides a seamless experience with premium design.

This project is developed as a graduation work, showcasing the possibilities of modern web technologies in the field of accessibility.
